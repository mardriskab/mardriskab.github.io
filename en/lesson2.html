HOW IT WORKS

TECHNOLOGY

How the simulation works

🧠

Multi-agent system

1000+ independent “agents” simultaneously model different future states — economic shocks, rate changes, personal life events, and market trends.

📊

Stress-test any question

Just type your question and press the “Simulate” button. The model automatically builds thousands of scenarios and finds the most stable profitable path.

🎯

The winning answer

After 1000 scenarios the system selects the highest-probability successful strategy, shows risks, and gives concrete next steps.

EXAMPLE

“What happens if I buy an apartment today?”

The user asks the question and activates simulation.

The system runs 1000 scenarios taking into account rate hikes, inflation, real-estate crashes, income changes, loan terms, etc.

You receive not one opinion, but the most probable and stable financial path with exact numbers and risk levels.

SIMULATION RESULT (EXAMPLE)
Best-performing scenario 68%
Buy 2-room apartment with 15% down payment, 15-year mortgage
High-risk scenarios 19%
Sharp rate increase + income drop
Recommendation: Wait 4-6 months or choose a smaller apartment
